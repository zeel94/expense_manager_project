Data according to Category and SubCategory


The categories of expenses can vary depending on the specific context or classification system used. However, here are some common categories of expenses:

Transportation: Vehicle expenses (fuel, maintenance, insurance), public transportation fares, taxi or ride-sharing services.

Food: Groceries, dining out, delivery services.

Entertainment: Movie tickets, concerts, sporting events, hobbies, subscriptions (streaming services, magazines).

Travel: Airfare, accommodation, transportation (car rentals, taxis), meals.

Education: Tuition fees, books, supplies.

Clothing: Apparel, shoes, accessories.

Utilities: Internet, cable/satellite TV, phone bills.

Insurance: Life insurance, home insurance, car insurance.

Taxes: Income taxes, property taxes.

Gifts and Donations: Presents for others, charitable contributions.

Childcare: Daycare expenses, babysitting services.

Pet Care: Veterinary bills, pet food, grooming.

Subscriptions: Newspaper/magazine subscriptions, online services.

Home Furnishings: Furniture, appliances, home decor.

Repairs and Maintenance: Vehicle repairs, appliance repairs, home repairs.

Business Expenses: Office supplies, advertising, business travel.

Home Improvement: Renovations, upgrades, remodeling.

Entertainment Subscriptions: Streaming services (Netflix, Spotify, etc.), gaming subscriptions.

Events and Celebrations: Parties, weddings, special occasions.

Electronics and Gadgets: Computers, smartphones, tablets, electronic accessories.

Fuel: Petrol,Deisel,Gas.

Others: Other expenses that do not fit into specific categories.
